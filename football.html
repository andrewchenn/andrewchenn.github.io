Football

Info:

Football has been a major part of my life since I was 8, where I started playing for my local club, Wallsend Red Devils. I remember going in goals for the first match I played and I must have performed really well, because I ended up as the goalkeeper for the rest of the season. In 2016 I got selected to join the Newcastle Football Summer Program, where I then was fortunate enough to earn a place at Hamilton Olympic in their NET squads for the next 2 years. My last year at Hamilton Olympic was in 2018, where I was dropped from the club after a series of poor performances the prior season. I needed to improve my distribution extensively if I even wanted a chance to play at NPL level the next year, so during that off-season, I worked as hard as I could with the help of Jets Academy goalkeeper coach, Andrew (Goldie) Goldman, to earn a place in next years competition. I ended up making it into the Charlestown City Blues u13 squad for the 2019 season and I regained my confidence to play at a high level. Then, following a series of good performances near the end of the season, I received an offer from Goldie to trial at the Jets Academy to potentially play in their u13 National Youth Championship squad. After some more good performances, I was selected and made the 5 hour journey down to Coffs Harbour to play against the best of my age group. Despite not doing so well at Nationals, It was deemed that I showed some good qualities during that tournament and was then selected to play for the Jets Academy for the next years and I have been their ever since. This year I am still at the Jets Academy, however I am out on loan to Valentine Eleebana FC in hopes to get more quality playing time. I am training twice a week with Jets and twice a week with Valo in the hopes of getting the most improvement.
